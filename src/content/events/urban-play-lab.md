We will head outside and nurture creative movement, building upon the capacity of the individual, the collective and the space. We do this with attention to expression, curiosity, space and personal boundaries. It will touch points like:

- Relationality to space and to others
- Learning to be visible and to express
- Nurturing creativity, improvisation
- Cultivating initiative and positioning yourself to others’ initiative
- Co-creation (games, challenges, flows, … the group will fuel the outcome)
